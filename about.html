Justin B. Gornell, PT, DPT, OCS

Justin B. Gornell  graduated from the College of the Holy Cross in 1997 with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and a concentration in Biological-Psychology. He graduated from New York University in 2001 as a Doctor of Physical Therapy. Justin became board certified in orthopedics (OCS - orthopedic clinical specialist) in 2005.


Justin has been working with orthopedic and sports-related injuries, as well as making house calls for years. He assists New York University's Physical Therapy program as a master clinician and has recently become one of the first members of TRIARQ. TRIARQ is a network of healthcare professionals creating new standards of excellence through continuing education, research and a cross discipline collaboration. He is an active member of the American Physical Therapy Association (APTA) and their Orthopedic Section, regularly keeping up with current research.


Justin enjoys running, working out and skiing, as well as spending time with his wife and four young boys.
